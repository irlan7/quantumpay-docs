# 🦅 QuantumChain L1: The Sovereign Compliance Blockchain

QuantumChain is a high-performance Layer-1 blockchain engineered for absolute regulatory compliance, specifically designed for the Indonesian ecosystem (OJK/Bappebti standards). 

Unlike traditional networks, QuantumChain features a **Native L1 Tax Engine** that automatically calculates and settles VAT (PPN) and Crypto Income Tax (PPh) on the consensus level, entirely eliminating manual tax reporting.

## 🌟 Core Features (100% Open Source)
- **Immutable State Machine**: Transparent account balances and nonces.
- **Native Tax Engine**: Hardcoded math for 11% VAT and 0.1% Crypto Tax. No hidden fees.
- **QRC-20 Standard**: The official standard for Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization and Sovereign CBDCs.
- **Trustless**: Verify the math yourself. The consensus engine is open for the world to see.
